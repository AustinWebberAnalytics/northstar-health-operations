\# Vendor Performance KPI Framework  



\## Northstar Health Operations



\---



\# Purpose



This document defines the operational KPI framework used within the Vendor Performance subsystem of the Northstar Health Operations ecosystem.



The purpose of this framework is to:



\- standardize vendor operational performance measurement

\- support fulfillment reliability monitoring

\- improve operational visibility

\- identify supplier instability trends

\- monitor escalation contribution activity

\- support leadership reporting

\- strengthen operational continuity analysis

\- support future SQL and Power BI integration



This framework serves as the primary operational measurement reference for vendor-related analytical reporting across the Northstar Health Operations ecosystem.

\# Fulfillment Reliability KPIs



Fulfillment Reliability KPIs evaluate vendor consistency in supporting operational inventory continuity.



\---



\## On-Time Delivery Rate



\### Purpose



Measures the percentage of shipments delivered within expected operational delivery windows.



\### Operational Relevance



Low on-time delivery performance may contribute to:



\- replenishment instability

\- operational shortages

\- workflow delays

\- escalation growth



\### Future Formula Example



```text

(On-Time Shipments / Total Shipments) × 100

```



\---



\## Fulfillment Accuracy Rate



\### Purpose



Measures shipment quantity and order accuracy across vendor deliveries.



\### Operational Relevance



Low fulfillment accuracy may contribute to:



\- inventory discrepancies

\- replenishment inefficiency

\- operational instability

\- increased reconciliation workload



\### Future Formula Example



```text

(Accurate Shipments / Total Shipments) × 100

```
