# Workforce Dataset Framework



## Purpose



This document defines the datasets that support the Workforce Coordination subsystem.



The objective is to provide visibility into staffing capacity, workload distribution, operational coverage, workforce utilization, and staffing-related operational risks.



Each dataset contributes to a specific aspect of workforce planning and operational decision-making.



---



## Dataset Architecture



The Workforce Coordination subsystem is supported by five primary datasets:



1. Workforce Roster

2. Workforce Assignments

3. Coverage Schedule

4. Workload Distribution

5. Staffing Escalations



Together, these datasets provide visibility into workforce availability, operational demand, workload balancing, and staffing-related risks.



---



## Workforce Roster



Purpose:



Provides a reference list of operational personnel and their organizational assignments.



Key Questions Supported:



- Who is assigned to each team?

- What roles exist within the workforce?

- Which department does each employee support?

- What staffing resources are currently available?



---



## Workforce Assignments



Purpose:



Tracks work assignments and responsibilities allocated to personnel.



Key Questions Supported:



- Who is responsible for specific operational activities?

- How is work distributed across personnel?

- Are assignments balanced across the team?

- Where are workload concentrations occurring?



---



## Coverage Schedule



Purpose:



Tracks planned workforce coverage across operational teams and shifts.



Key Questions Supported:



- Are staffing levels sufficient for planned operations?

- Where are coverage gaps occurring?

- Which teams may require additional support?

- What periods have the greatest staffing constraints?



---



## Workload Distribution



Purpose:



Measures workload allocation across teams and personnel.



Key Questions Supported:



- Which teams are experiencing the highest workload pressure?

- Where are workload imbalances occurring?

- How effectively is available staffing capacity being utilized?

- Which areas may require workload redistribution?



---



## Staffing Escalations



Purpose:



Tracks workforce-related operational issues requiring management attention.



Key Questions Supported:



- Where are staffing shortages affecting operations?

- Which departments are experiencing recurring workforce issues?

- What workforce risks require escalation?

- Which staffing challenges have the greatest operational impact?



---



## Dataset Relationships



The Workforce Roster serves as the primary workforce reference dataset.



Workforce Assignments, Coverage Schedule, Workload Distribution, and Staffing Escalations all reference workforce personnel, teams, and departments to provide a complete view of operational workforce conditions.



Together, these datasets support reporting, KPI development, workload analysis, capacity planning, and workforce risk monitoring.



---



## Expected Analytical Outcomes



These datasets are designed to support analysis of:



- Staffing capacity

- Workforce utilization

- Coverage adequacy

- Workload balancing

- Staffing-related operational risk

- Workforce escalation trends



The goal is to provide actionable workforce intelligence that supports informed operational planning and resource allocation decisions.
