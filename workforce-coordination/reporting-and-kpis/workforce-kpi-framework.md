# Workforce KPI Framework



## Purpose



This document defines the key performance indicators used to measure workforce capacity, utilization, coverage effectiveness, workload distribution, and staffing-related operational risk.



These metrics support workforce planning, operational decision-making, and resource allocation activities.



---



## Workforce Capacity KPIs



### Active Workforce Count



Definition:



Total number of active employees available to support operational activities.



Business Value:



Provides visibility into available workforce capacity.



Source Dataset:



- workforce-roster



---



### Workforce Availability Rate



Definition:



Percentage of workforce records classified as active.



Calculation:



Active Employees ÷ Total Employees



Business Value:



Measures overall workforce availability.



Source Dataset:



- workforce-roster



---



## Coverage KPIs



### Coverage Fulfillment Rate



Definition:



Percentage of scheduled coverage assignments successfully covered.



Calculation:



Covered Schedules ÷ Total Schedules



Business Value:



Measures workforce coverage effectiveness.



Source Dataset:



- workforce-coverage-schedule



---



### Uncovered Shift Count



Definition:



Total number of uncovered workforce schedules.



Business Value:



Identifies potential operational coverage risks.



Source Dataset:



- workforce-coverage-schedule



---



## Workload KPIs



### Capacity Utilization Rate



Definition:



Average workforce capacity utilization percentage.



Business Value:



Measures how effectively workforce resources are being utilized.



Source Dataset:



- workforce-workload-distribution



---



### Overloaded Employee Count



Definition:



Total number of employees classified as overloaded.



Business Value:



Identifies potential staffing constraints and workload imbalances.



Source Dataset:



- workforce-workload-distribution



---



### Underutilized Employee Count



Definition:



Total number of employees classified as underutilized.



Business Value:



Highlights opportunities for workload redistribution.



Source Dataset:



- workforce-workload-distribution



---



## Assignment KPIs



### Active Assignment Count



Definition:



Total number of active workforce assignments.



Business Value:



Measures operational workload commitments.



Source Dataset:



- workforce-assignments



---



### Cross-Functional Assignment Rate



Definition:



Percentage of assignments supporting multiple operational areas.



Calculation:



Cross-Functional Assignments ÷ Total Assignments



Business Value:



Measures workforce flexibility and coordination requirements.



Source Dataset:



- workforce-assignments



---



## Workforce Risk KPIs



### Open Escalation Count



Definition:



Total number of workforce escalations that remain unresolved.



Business Value:



Measures active workforce-related operational risk.



Source Dataset:



- workforce-escalations



---



### High Severity Escalation Count



Definition:



Total number of workforce escalations classified as High or Critical severity.



Business Value:



Highlights workforce issues requiring management attention.



Source Dataset:



- workforce-escalations



---



## Reporting Objectives



This KPI framework is designed to support visibility into:



- Workforce capacity

- Staffing availability

- Coverage effectiveness

- Workload distribution

- Workforce utilization

- Staffing-related operational risk



The goal is to provide actionable workforce intelligence that supports informed operational planning and resource allocation decisions.
