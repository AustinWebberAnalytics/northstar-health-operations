**Writing Layer:** Layer 1 — Reader-Facing

## [Phase 2 Reviews](phase-2-reviews/README.md)

---
# Authority Boundary

Reviews explain findings, decisions, and validation history.

Current approved Governance and System Design documents remain authoritative. A review does not override a locked artifact unless the resulting change has been approved and incorporated into that artifact.
