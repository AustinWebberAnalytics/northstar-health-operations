# Repository Remediation Plan



## Purpose



This document defines the execution plan for Phase II repository standardization and remediation activities.



The objective is to apply approved standards consistently across the Northstar ecosystem while minimizing disruption, preserving subsystem integrity, and reducing the risk of introducing new inconsistencies.



This plan converts Phase II review findings into an actionable implementation roadmap.



---



# Phase II Mission



The purpose of remediation is not to expand the repository.



The purpose is to improve:



* Consistency

* Readability

* Maintainability

* Scalability

* Professional presentation



Phase II prioritizes standardization over growth.



---



# Remediation Philosophy



The Northstar ecosystem should be updated deliberately and systematically.



Changes should be applied according to approved standards rather than personal preference.



The goal is to eliminate inconsistency while preserving subsystem identity.



---



# Guiding Principles



## Principle 1



Standardize before expanding.



---



## Principle 2



Apply standards consistently across all subsystems.



---



## Principle 3

---



## Principle 4



Avoid introducing complexity that does not provide meaningful value.



---

```text

├── data/

└── schemas/

```

---



## Documentation Standards

```text

Reader Facing

Layer 3

Governance

```



---



## Reporting Standards



Approved:



```text

Executive Summary



Pivot Tables



Raw Data

```



Workbook order.



---



### Executive Summary Structure



Approved:



```text

Purpose



Reporting Period



KPI Highlights



Operational Observations



Reporting Confidence

```



---



### Pivot Standards



Approved:



* Business-friendly headers

* Operational sort order

* Grand Totals removed by default

* Concise observations



---



## Communication Standards



Approved:



* Clarity over sophistication

* Signal over noise

* Operational meaning over jargon

* Readability over complexity
