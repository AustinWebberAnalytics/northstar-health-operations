# Inventory Operations Subsystem Overview

## Purpose

The Inventory Operations subsystem supports the monitoring, replenishment, movement, and reconciliation of inventory across operational locations.

The subsystem provides visibility into inventory availability, shortage conditions, replenishment activity, shipment performance, and inventory discrepancies to support operational continuity and informed decision-making.

---

# Operational Objective

The primary objective of the Inventory Operations subsystem is to maintain appropriate inventory levels required to support uninterrupted organizational operations.

Key responsibilities include:

* Monitoring inventory availability
* Managing replenishment activity
* Coordinating inventory recovery efforts
* Supporting vendor shipment visibility
* Investigating inventory discrepancies
* Reducing operational disruption caused by inventory shortages

# Inventory Workflow Lifecycle

Typical inventory workflow progression:

```text
Inventory Monitoring
↓
Low Stock Identification
↓
Replenishment Request
↓
Shipment or Transfer Coordination
↓
Inventory Receipt
↓
Inventory Validation
↓
Inventory Reconciliation
↓
Operational Resolution
```

Some workflows may require escalation due to:

* Inventory shortages
* Shipment delays
* Inventory discrepancies
* Demand increases
* Vendor fulfillment issues

# Operational Summary

The Inventory Operations subsystem provides the operational structure required to monitor inventory health, coordinate replenishment activity, manage shortages, investigate discrepancies, and support inventory-related decision-making.

The subsystem serves as a foundational operational domain within the broader Northstar ecosystem and contributes to organizational visibility, operational continuity, and performance monitoring.
